Gekkou Recreation Project:

This is my side project to recreate the ROBLOX game "Gekkou Online" originally by GeckoHero17.
This game is very important to my friends and I, and as ROBLOX has updated their code, this game has fallen behind and is no longer functional.
I plan to bring a new life to this game, or to at least make a playable recreation for my friends to enjoy.
